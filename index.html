Coding In The Open

Coding In The Open is a week of 1 hour seminars happening June 14th - 18th, online.

We aim to bring the discussion around Open Source practices - that is, the collaboration across organisations to achieve bigger things - to a new audience.

We’ve some fantastic speakers lined up from public, private, and academic sectors to talk about what being “open” means to them, and how it improves their world.
